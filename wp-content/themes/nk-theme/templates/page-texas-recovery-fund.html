Required disclosure for Texas consumers.

Residential Mortgage Loan Originator Recovery Fund

The Texas Department of Savings and Mortgage Lending (TDSML) has established the Residential Mortgage Loan Originator Recovery Fund to provide restitution to Texas consumers who have suffered actual damages as a result of an act of a licensed residential mortgage loan originator that constitutes a violation of applicable law.

How to File a Complaint

If you believe you have been harmed by a residential mortgage loan originator, you may file a complaint with:

Recovery Fund Eligibility

To be eligible for recovery from the fund, you must:

  1. Be a Texas resident at the time of the transaction
  2. Have suffered actual damages as a result of an act of a licensed residential mortgage loan originator
  3. File a complaint within the applicable time periods
  4. Meet all other requirements established by the TDSML

Limitations

The recovery fund has certain limitations on the amount of recovery and the circumstances under which recovery is available. For complete information about the recovery fund, including eligibility requirements and limitations, please contact the Texas Department of Savings and Mortgage Lending.

Licensing Information
